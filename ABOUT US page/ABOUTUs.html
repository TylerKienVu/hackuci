HACKUCI 2018

Behind "Guess that Hashtag!"

 On February 2-4 of 2018, participants from all over California came together to hack at University of California - Irvine's 'HackUCI'. Students from California State University of Fullerton, University of California San Diego, Univesity of California - Riverside, especially from University of Calilfornia - Irvine, they all came to experience this wonderful event. 

 Juleanne Palad, Gevork Gabrielyan, Tyler Vu, Andrew Gomez, and Deone Peng (left to right), and Tyler Vu joined together to create a web development desktop app called 'Guess that Hashtag'. The game is similar to 'Four Pics One Word', where the user figures out what all the pictures have in common within a singular word. In 'Guess that Hashtag', the user must figure out what the given tweets have in common within an individual hashtag. Gabrielyan & Vu implemented Twitter API to gather Twitter's most popular hashtags while Gomez, Peng, & Palad created the game's home page, game page, and About Us page using HTML & CSS.
